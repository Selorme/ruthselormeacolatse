Welcome to My World!

Hello! I'm Ruth Selorme Acolatse, a passionate Ghanaian currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Journalism at Marmara University in Istanbul, Turkey. My journey brought me to Türkiye, and I’ve chronicled the experiences that led me here on my blog. Feel free to dive into my story and explore how this vibrant city has shaped my path.

Debate has been a significant part of my life. I've had the honor of judging the Open Grand Final of the World Universities Debating Championships twice (2023 & 2024), as well as the Grand Final of the World Schools Debating Championship twice in 2023 and 2024. These experiences have fueled my love for intellectual challenge and eloquence.

Public speaking is another arena where I strive to excel. I’m proud to have earned the title of World's Public Speaking Runner-Up for 2024, a testament to my dedication and passion for compelling communication.

Travel is close to my heart. According to my Google Maps history, I've circled the globe more than twice and visited 24 countries. My travels have been a source of inspiration and adventure, enriching my life in countless ways.

Currently, I'm delving into the world of programming, learning Python with hopes of becoming a successful programmer. Stay tuned for updates on my coding journey and discoveries here!

Now that I've shared a glimpse of my world, explore the menu to discover more about me and my endeavors. Grab a snack, settle in, and enjoy your visit!
